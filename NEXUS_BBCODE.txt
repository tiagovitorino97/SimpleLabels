[size=4][b]What Is It?[/b][/size]
Simple Labels lets you add [b]custom text labels[/b] to storage units and crafting stations so you can see at a glance what's where. Labels also show on your [b]clipboard[/b], so your production line stays clear and organized without breaking immersion. Works with [b]all station types[/b] and storage closet variants, with a [b]clean, in-game look[/b] that fits the game.
